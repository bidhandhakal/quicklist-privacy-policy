Privacy Policy for QuickList

Last Updated: December 9, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to QuickList ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains how the QuickList mobile application (the "App") handles your information.

QuickList is designed to function without collecting, storing, or processing any personal data from users.

Information I Collect

I do not collect, store, or process any personal information from users. All tasks, notes, and settings you create remain on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

QuickList uses Google AdMob to display ads. While I do not collect any data, Google AdMob may collect device-related information for advertising purposes. This data is processed directly by Google and not by me.

  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (non-precise)
  • Device information needed to serve ads
  • Ad interaction data

You can learn more at: Google Privacy Policy

How I Use Your Information

Since I do not collect any personal data, no user information is stored or used by the App.

The only data processed is handled by Google AdMob for displaying ads.

Data Storage

Your task lists, notes, and preferences are stored locally on your device only. I do not upload or store any information on external servers.

AdMob data, if collected, is stored and processed by Google according to their policy.

Data Sharing

I do not share, sell, or transfer any personal information because none is collected.

The only third party involved is Google AdMob, which may process limited device information for ad delivery.

Your Rights and Choices

Since no personal data is collected by the App:

  • There is no personal data to access, change, or delete
  • Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored task data

Advertising Preferences

  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings
  • You can reset your advertising ID at any time

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Security

Since no data is collected on external servers, your privacy is protected by design. All data is stored only on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
